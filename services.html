Our Services

Metaphor Media delivers end-to-end
experiential, shopper, and promotional solutions designed to drive engagement, conversion, and measurable impact—powered by creativity, technology, and AI.

Experiential Marketing

We create immersive brand experiences that connect emotionally and perform commercially.

  • Live, hybrid & virtual experiences
  • Brand activations, pop-ups & roadshows
  • Product launches & brand immersions
  • Interactive & sensory experiences
Events & Conferences

From intimate leadership meetings to global-scale events, we deliver seamless execution.

  • Conferences, summits & forums
  • Corporate & brand events
  • Trade shows, exhibitions & expos
  • Event design, production & management
Shopper & Retail Marketing

We influence decision-making at the moment it matters most.

  • Shopper journey & conversion strategy
  • In-store & omnichannel activations
  • POS, visual merchandising & retail design
  • Field marketing & brand ambassador programs
Promotional Marketing

We drive trial, loyalty, and repeat purchase through meaningful engagement.

  • Consumer & trade promotions
  • Sampling & experiential trial
  • Incentive, loyalty & gamified campaigns
Digital, Immersive & Smart Technology

Technology-enhanced experiences that elevate engagement.

  • AR, VR & immersive environments
  • Interactive digital installations
  • NFC, QR, RFID & touchless solutions
  • Experiential platforms & microsites
AI - Powered Marketing Solutions

Smarter experiences driven by data and intelligence.

  • AI-driven consumer insights & analytics
  • Personalization at scale
  • AI-powered lead capture & qualification
  • Conversational AI & event chatbots
  • Real-time optimization & performance tracking
Strategy, Data & Measurement

Every experience is designed to deliver results.

  • Brand & experience strategy
  • Consumer & shopper journey mapping
  • ROI measurement & reporting dashboards
  • Behavioral insights & attribution modeling
Creative & Production

Bold ideas brought to life with precision.

  • Creative concepting & storytelling
  • Spatial & experience design
  • Content creation & technical production
